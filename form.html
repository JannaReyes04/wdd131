Meycauayan 2nd Ward Website Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Meycauayan 2nd Ward, Valenzuela Stake

Description: This name clearly identifies the website as belonging to the Meycauayan 2nd Ward, a local congregation of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, within the Valenzuela Stake. It is straightforward and informative, indicating the specific community and religious organization the site represents.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a central online resource for the members of the Meycauayan 2nd Ward and for individuals interested in learning more about the ward. It aims to:

  • Provide up-to-date information on ward activities, events, and announcements.
  • Facilitate communication and connection among ward members.
  • Offer a welcoming and informative introduction to the ward for visitors and investigators.
  • Share the history and background of the ward.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: For a Ward Member

Question: "I'm a member of the ward. How can I find out the schedule for this week's activities and meetings, including any changes to the regular schedule?"

Answer: The website will feature a prominent and regularly updated calendar of events. Members can view the calendar on the homepage or a dedicated "Calendar" page, with options to filter by organization (Relief Society, Priesthood, etc.). Any changes to the schedule, such as special meetings or cancellations, will be clearly indicated in the announcements section and on the calendar.

Scenario 2: For an Investigator

Question: "I am interested in learning more about the Meycauayan 2nd Ward and The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Where can I find basic information about your beliefs and meeting times, and who can I contact for further inquiries?"

Answer: The website will provide a welcoming introduction on the homepage, with links to information about the Church's basic beliefs and practices. The "Contact Us" section will provide contact information for ward leaders, and meeting times and location will be clearly displayed. The site may also include links to the official Church website (ChurchofJesusChrist.org) for more comprehensive information.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #003366 (Dark Blue)
    • Used for: Headings (h1, h2, h3), strong accents, and possibly some text links for emphasis.
  • Secondary Color: #A93226 (Dark Red)
    • Used for: Links, and to highlight important information or calls to action.
  • Background Color: #F2F4F8 (Very Light Gray)
    • Used for: The main background color of the website, providing a clean and neutral base.
  • Accent Color: #D4A373 (Sandy Brown)
    • Used for: Highlights, buttons, and visual elements to draw attention.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Inter
    • Used for: Body text, paragraphs, lists, and general content to ensure readability.
  • Secondary Font: Montserrat
    • Used for: Headings (h1, h2, h3) to provide a distinct and modern look.
